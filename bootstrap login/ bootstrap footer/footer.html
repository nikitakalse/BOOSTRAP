Google

Google is one of the leading information technology (IT) companies in the world and is recognized worldwide for its innovative technology solutions and consulting services. It was founded on September 4, 1998, by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. The company is headquartered in Mountain View, California. Over the years, Google has grown from a small startup into a global organization serving clients in more than 50 countries.
